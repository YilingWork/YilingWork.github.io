About · 學經歷

從產業第一線，到政策研究現場From the industry frontline to the policy research floor

蔡懿玲擁有藥學、企業管理與醫療科技評估的多元背景。她的職涯橫跨臨床、產業與學術：曾任醫院藥師，並在美商、日商、歐商跨國藥廠累積十餘年經驗，歷任業務、行銷、市場准入與政府事務要職，接觸學名藥、生物相似藥、新成分新藥與癌症次世代基因檢測。如今她把這份第一線的產業視角，帶進健康政策的研究與教學。Yi-Ling Tsai brings together pharmacy, business management, and health technology assessment. Her career spans clinic, industry, and academia — a former hospital pharmacist with 15+ years across US, Japanese, and European pharma companies, holding senior roles in sales, marketing, market access, and government affairs. She now carries that frontline perspective into health-policy research and teaching.

Research · 研究

研究興趣與著作Research interests & works

聚焦藥品政策、醫療科技評估與病人賦權，以真實世界資料為健保決策提供實證。Focused on pharmaceutical policy, HTA, and patient empowerment — grounding reimbursement decisions in real-world evidence.

政

藥品政策Pharmaceutical Policy

新藥給付決策機制、定價／創新／可近性的平衡，罕藥與精準醫療的政策挑戰。New-drug reimbursement mechanisms; the balance of pricing, innovation and access; orphan drugs and precision medicine.

HTA

醫療科技評估Health Technology Assessment

預算衝擊分析與成本效益分析，評估新藥與新科技納入健保的經濟影響。Budget impact and cost-effectiveness analyses of new drugs and technologies entering national insurance.

賦

病人賦權Patient Empowerment

病人組織參與政策、用藥與健康識能量表、電子仿單（ePI）與多元族群資訊可近性。Patient participation in policy, medication/health literacy tools, and electronic package inserts (ePI) for diverse populations.
